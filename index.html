GIRAFFES

Giraffes are from the heartland of Africa. They are only located in the African countries of:
Kenya, Cameroon, Chad, Niger, Uganda, Namibia, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Tanzania,
Angola, and South Africa. *Many are rescued and are brought to WildLife and Game Reserves.*
